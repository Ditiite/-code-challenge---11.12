The main building of Santa Claus Holiday Village’ (also called “Christmas House”) hosts unique Christmas-theme exhibition and a photography service with Santa Claus.
The exhibition includes two fascinating exhibits covering around 400 m²: / Read More /

Finnish Christmas, Christmas traditions and Lapland’s Santa Claus, “Joulupukki” Most recognized Christmas traditions around the WorldThe exhibition covers today’s Christmas customs as well as centuries-old Finnish and foreign Christmas traditions. The permanent exhibit includes also fascinating Christmas videos.
Photography service: At Christmas House you also have the opportunity to meet Santa Claus, and you can create a lasting memory of the meeting with a high quality photograph.
